## 🗳️ **Voting System**

### How to Vote:
1. Click the **"Vote on Itinerary"** button on the homepage
2. Choose a category (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Activities, or Nightlife)
3. **Select your name** from the dropdown (this is important!)
4. Click on your favorite options:
   - **Meals (Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner):** 1 vote per category
   - **Activities:** Up to 2 votes per day
   - **Nightlife:** Up to 3 votes
5. Click **"Submit Your Vote"**
6. Your vote is instantly saved and shared with all 16 Pearls!

### View Results:
- Click **"View Results"** on the voting hub page
- See real-time vote counts for each option
- Winners are highlighted with trophy icons 🏆
- Refresh anytime to see updated results

## 📸 **Photo Album**

Share your Nashville memories with all your line sisters!

### How to Upload Photos:
1. Click **"Photo Album"** on the homepage
2. Select your name
3. **Add a photo URL or Google Drive link:**
   - For Google Drive: Share the file → Copy link → Paste here
   - For direct image URLs: Paste the URL
4. (Optional) Select which day the photo is from
5. (Optional) Add a caption
6. Click **"✨ Add Photo"**

### Viewing Photos:
- All photos appear in the grid below
- Filter by day using the buttons at the top
- Delete your own photos if needed

**💡 Pro Tip:** Create a shared Google Drive folder for the trip and share photos there!

## 💰 **Split Payment Tracker**

No more "who owes who" confusion!

### Adding an Expense:
1. Click **"Split Payments"** on the homepage
2. Select your name to see your personal summary
3. Click **"Add New Expense"**
4. Fill in the details:
   - What was it for? (e.g., "Airbnb", "Dinner at Hattie B's")
   - Total amount (e.g., $1,200)
   - Who paid?
   - Who should split the cost? (Select all that apply or use "Select All")
5. Click **"✨ Add Expense"**

### Marking Payments:
- When you've paid someone back, find that expense in the list
- Click **"Mark Paid"** next to your name
- The expense will automatically update to "Settled" once everyone has paid

### Your Personal Summary:
- Select your name at the top to see:
  - **You Owe:** Total amount you need to pay to others
  - **You Are Owed:** Total amount others need to pay you
  - Detailed breakdown of each expense

### Example:
```
Airbnb: $1,600
Paid by: Pearl 1
Split among: All 16 Pearls
= $100 each

Everyone except Pearl 1 owes her $100
Pearl 1 is owed $1,500 total
```
