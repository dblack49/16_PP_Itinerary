A split payments tracker is basically a shared ledger for the trip so everybody can see:

what was paid for

who paid

who still owes

how much each person owes (and to whom)

It replaces the chaos of “send me your portion” + 40 messages + someone always forgetting they already paid 😭

How it works on your Nashville trip

Let’s say you have 8 line sisters.

1) You log each shared expense

Examples:

Airbnb deposit

Party bus

Dinner reservation deposit

Groceries / bottles

Ubers (if you decide to split)

Each expense entry includes:

What it was (“Party Bus”)

Total cost ($640)

Who paid (Derienne)

Who’s included (all 8 or a subset)

Split method (even split, custom, or percentages)

Status (open/settled)

Optional: receipt photo

2) The tracker calculates what everyone owes

For the party bus example:

$640 / 8 people = $80 each

Since you paid, it shows:

Everyone owes you $80

You owe $0 (you already covered it)

3) It produces a “who owes who” summary

You’ll have a dashboard like:

Kia → owes Derienne $80 (Party Bus)

Jade → owes Derienne $80 (Party Bus)

…

And a per-person total:

“Jade owes: $120 total”

“Derienne is owed: $560 total”

4) People mark payments as they pay you back

They can tap:

“Paid” (and optionally attach Zelle/Cash App screenshot)

Or you, as admin, mark them paid

Once everyone pays, that expense becomes Settled.

What makes it better than a notes app

It auto-calculates splits

You can split expenses among only some people (ex: 5 people went to a brunch)

It keeps one source of truth

It prevents double-paying and “I thought I paid already”

Quick example flow (realistic)

Friday

Airbnb: $1,200 paid by Nia, split among 8 → $150 each

Grocery run: $160 paid by you, split among 6 → $26.67 each

Rooftop section deposit: $300 paid by Jade, split among 8 → $37.50 each

The tracker tells each person:

What they owe + to whom
And tells each payer:

How much they’re owed total
